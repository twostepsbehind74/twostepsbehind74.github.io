About

TwoStepsBehind is a small, living project — short texts, reflections, and quiet signals gathered along the road.

I’m building this in public, but not as a performance. I write to leave a trace — honest enough to be real, simple enough to share.

A note on tools: I use AI as a practical assistant while I write. The ideas, direction, and final choices are mine — AI helps me shape language, stay consistent, and keep momentum when my written language gets heavy.
